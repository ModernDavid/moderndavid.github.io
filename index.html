ABOUT

Modern David is a social media ministry dedicated to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and teachings of the Bible in a very easy way. Led by Matthew Paul. Our mission is to bring the message of hope, love, and salvation to a digital generation. In this fast-paced world, we understand the need to reach people where they are, and social media provides a platform for us to connect, engage, and encourage a global community seeking spiritual growth and guidance.
At Modern David, we embrace a modern approach to faith while staying grounded in the timeless truths of the Bible. We aim to bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and contemporary challenges, presenting biblical teachings in a way that resonates with today's audience. Our content is designed to inspire, challenge, and encourage personal growth and spiritual development.
We invite you to join our community and embark on a journey of faith and discovery. Follow us on our social media channels, engage in meaningful conversations, and share our content with others who may benefit from it. Together, we can be a modern generation of Davids, facing the giants of life with unwavering faith, courage, and the love of God.
Thank you for visiting Modern David. We look forward to connecting with you and witnessing the transformative power of God's Word in your life.
Blessings, Matthew Paul Founder, Modern David
